PROBLEM STATEMENT

A majority of the young adult generation are affected by loneliness, boredom and unproductive lifestyle due to the lockdown of pandemic. This situation can be much worse for the single person living alone at one place for long.

PROJECT GOALS

The high level goals were to -

  • Create personal assistant experience to provide empathetic interaction
  • Create interactive user interface to improve user engagement and user’s productivity
  • Create a new visual appearance of Amazon Alexa by integrate Alexa voice assistant and mobile app to provide delightful experience to user

BACKGROUND

Amazon's Alexa is one of the popular of digital AI assistant. Alexa has the potential to serve the user more efficiently, as it is already well appreciated by user for its amazing household task. Due to the lack of interactive communication with Alexa rather than the voice command, people are mostly using it for controlling smart home devices. Adding the non-verbal communication with Alexa along with verbal communication and responsibilities like personal assistant can increase the user engagement. The concept comes from my post graduate research on analyzing user engagement with virtual AI assistant. Having both verbal and non-verbal communication interactive visual appearance with gestures and emotion along with the voice) can improve the engagement during the user interaction

I began my research by looking at the research paper, article and statistics. I wanted to understand their experience with AI assistant, emotion, and effect in their daily life.

  • 2 in 5 adults use voice search once daily. (Location World)

In a nutshell young adults are the main consumers of voice assistant and they have very intimate experiences with smart devices.

SURVEY STUDY

I have collected data from 15 people through E-survey. The participants were from 19-30 years old, use Amazon Alexa (may use google home also) and single person at home. Key findings:


  1. 45% participants reported Alexa's conversation flow is not very user friendly and interactive.
  2. 57% participants reported they would like to see more interactive facility in the mobile app other than voice command also.

KEY TAKEWAYS

From the research I think that coming out just from the traditional usage, by including interactive and empathetic behavioral features to the traditional Amazon Alexa app can increase the engagement with Alexa as personal assistant. The goal of the new empathetic and interactive features of Alexa is to help on making the user interaction more engaging and interesting in boredom or loneliness.

DESIGN

WIREFRAMES

PROTOTYPE

Feature 1: Home Screen Dashboard

User can customize their favorite cards on the home screen dashboard unlike the traiditional Alexa app home screen. Inlcuding meaningful and necessary contents of user's to the front page can improve the user engagement.

The "edit" icon allows user to add and delete cards in the dashboard. They can also reorder the cards.

Feature 2: News and Information collection

The News and Information feature is more interactive and insightful now. The updated feature will show collection of contents based on catagory and give the option to read content inside the app without redirecting to external link. This feature will be helpful if user is not in a mood to use voice command and they don't need to ask about the same news information again and again.

Users can choose their catagory they want to see in the collection from the setting option "...", order catagory acccording to their priority. Additionally, they can pin favourite catagory from "add to favourite" icon. This will help the user to have the insight and foresight for their relevant field.

Feature 3: Personalized Mood

The personalized mood of Alexa AI provides empathetic feature controlling smart home devices to create personalized environment for user accroding to users mood.

Depending on user command, It connects with home smart devices, such as Nest Thermostat, Philips Hue, or Smart plugs, and controls the color, lighting dimmer, temperature AC/ Fan, and music playlists depends on the user’s current feeling to make their comfortable environment.

Feature 4: Story and Joke

Rater than just displaying text on the joke screen, A 3D animated character (including voice, gestures, expressions and lip synchronization) will tell Jokes in the joke screen. Same as for story.

Only reading or listening joke from a voice that has no expressions in the voice is not enough make things funny. Watching a animated character telling joke with releavant expression let user to fun with Alexa AI.

Feature 5: Movie and Music

The updated movie feature is more interactive now. User can view their most watched and recently watched movie list from here. Thus, user don't need to ask to give the same type of movie recomendation again and again. They can choose from the list directly.

The updated music feature stores users recent and most listened playlist and play from here according to user command. Thus, user don't need to ask for music recomendation again and again for same type of music.

USABILITY TESTING

From the the usabilty testing, participants have mentioned some issues with the app and I have fixed those in the final prototype.

Problems

  1. Widget editing was not closing while clicking on the app.
  2. Previewed movie was not highlighted.
  3. Drag and drop option of widget isn’t smooth.
  4. Virtual character background is not transparent with app background

Solutions

  1. Editing preview closed while clicking outside of the widget.
  2. Made the displayed movie card larger and overlayed than others.
  3. Made the drag and drop transition smooth.
  4. Work in progress

SUMMARY ABOUT ME

MY STORY

My name is Procheta Nag and I’m passionate about creating digital experiences with an impact on users' lives. I have recently completed my HCI Master’s degree within 1 year 10 months from Simon Fraser University. I have a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering. Through the learning I realized that a good product is a true creation of good design, experience and technology.

My interest in human-centered design made me pursue my career in UX Design. Having a strong user experience research background gave me advantages to amalgame user research into design.

Outside of study, being an organizer of Google Developers Group and Women Techmakers has given a platform for technical knowledge sharing.

LEARNING CURVE
I learned and practiced human-centered design during my masters study.
From my thirst to learn more about design, I was only doing teaching assistant of the design related courses for two years. I have learnt a lot of UX/UI design knowledge while teaching courses like Information Design and Digital Image Design.
I spent a lot of time doing design courses like user experience nanodegree(udacity) and in coursera, reading books, exploring online and applying these to make projects
